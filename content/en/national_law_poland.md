* **Legal sanctions in cases of parental abduction**
  Criminal Code: article 211 — Act of 6 June 1997.

* **Parental authority and visitation rights**
  Family Code: articles 87 to 113(6).

* **Procedure for the return of the child**
  Procedural Act of 17 November 1964.

* **Regulations concerning the Central Authority**
  There are also certain regulations concerning the conduct of the Central Authority in cases falling under the Hague Convention, in particular the Act of 26 January 2018.
